# Frequency Drawer

**Latest version:** 1.0 — download builds from the [Releases](../../../../releases) page.

## Manual

Frequency Drawer VST is an experimental plugin with a canvas on which you can draw sound that
is played back from left to right.

It remains a draft version, as my approach to render sound after each stroke is quite time-consuming. The resulting sound is very experimental too.

If you are willing to invest time in it, it can produce some interesting tones for sound design though.

There are two sound creation engines, engine 1 also supports the interpolated drawing mode.

After or during drawing, you can set a frequency blur reverb and amount of harmonics.
You can click and drag the playhead in playhead mode and export to FLAC.
