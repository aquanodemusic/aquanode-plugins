## Manual

SpectralEnhance

The controls are exactly the same as in SpectralGate, but rather than deleting frequencies we take them towards the level of the gate line! It thus acts like a spectral brickwall compressor, either upwards or downwards at a time (but you can of course always use two instances of these plugins in your DAW to achieve both effects at the same time).
