# SpectralCompare

Spectral Compare lets you view a spectrogram of the sound you play on the track you place the effect on,
and overlays a sidechained sound spectrogram on top of it.

Setup: 
Route Sound Source 1 to Track A,
Route Sound Source 2 to Track B
Sidechain Track B to Track A (without volume, only the inaudible sidechain),
Place Spectral Compare on Track A,
Enable Sidechain input for Spectral Compare in your DAW (depends on the DAW).
